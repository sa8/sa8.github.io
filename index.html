I am the founder of Kubi, a decentralized infrastructure protocol for subscription platforms for creators and their fans that fights unfair deplatforming and censorship. I am also building Kinba, a client built on top of Kubi.

I have been designing and building decentralised systems for almost 8 years. I have a PhD in Computer Science from University College London and I have worked on the security of the Filecoin consensus protocol from January 2020 (pre-launch) to February 2023.
